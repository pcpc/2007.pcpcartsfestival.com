Arts Festival Kickoff · Thursday February 1, 2007 by PCPC Webmaster

At the Arts Festival Kick-off and Demonstration held on November 19, a good time was had by all. Over 120 people were in attendance to watch as Bob Hogan (sculpture) and Shelley Kolman Smith (watercolor) created beautiful art and Bob Snider of Little Rock provided vision for the Festival and answered questions. There was even a surprise visit from a nearby Shepherd to give the artists an isiders view of Psalm 23.
